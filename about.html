About

Hi to the one on the other side of the screen !

I am Ronnie. Currently, I work as security researcher in a firm in Hyderabad, India; where my day to day work involves finding zero days in popular open source applications. I conduct both SAST and DAST analysis on various applications present in Github which covers a wide variety of ecosystems like NPM, Ruby, Nuget, PHP, Java etc and report findings to the project maintainers to get the vulnerability patched asap :P

My interest in computers started quite young, though I started to have a browsable internet connection way late in my Senior year in school. During those days, I was tinkering with the sole Android mobile in our house; rooting, installing custom ROM’s , throttling processors etc. I also started my YouTube Channel where I continue to upload interesting videos related to tech and hacking even now!

I pursued my B. Tech. in CSE from Karunya University, Coimbatore. During the freshman year, I developed keen interest in learning about SEO (Search engine optimisation) and started a blog, geekslider(.)com which I later sold to my college alumni.
In the upcoming years at college, I realized I had more interest into breaking applications, playing CTF and finding security loopholes. I coincidently found a security bug in a very popular image editing site; through which I was able to access their premium functionality for free, while I was trying to create logo for by blog.

The bug was patched and the site rewarded me under their “Bug Bounty Program”. That was the beginning of a wonderful journey in the infosec field and I reported security issues in a couple of sites. I have been acknowledged in the Security Hall of Fames of all Big 3 : Google, Facebook and Microsoft . You may please read more about that here.

While at my junior year in college, I was selected by Facebook, as one among 30 students from Asia-Pacific region to attend an invite only security conference and live hacking event, Bountycon 2019 in Singapore. The conference enabled me to interact with top security researchers worldwide.

I was also the first Microsoft Student Partner from my university; through which I organised seminars for students. Some of my talks/seminars are:

  • Making Apps using Google Assistants
  • Intro to Git and Github
  • Real World OSINT Hacking

Please read more of my technical works here.

I am also exploring varius OSINT data gathering techniques; and as a part in this direction, volunteered as judge in Trace Labs CTF, organized at Defcon USA, 2021 to aid in finding missing people.

I also volunteer at Kerala Police Cyberdome; which brings together Law Enforcement Agencies and industry experts from Public Domain for combating cyber crimes. I spend my free time reading novels and researching about upcoming cyber attacks. I also published my first book on Amazon, Grey Voice which is a collection of 10 short stories and poems.

By nature, I am a very simple person; and love to hangout with humble people.
